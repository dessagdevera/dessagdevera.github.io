iMonitor

As part of our capstone project, I led a team that developed an On-the-Job Training (OJT) monitoring system for our school, STI College Santa Maria. The system enabled communication between the Alumni and Placement Officer, OJT advisers, and student trainees. It allowed the student trainees to update their respective advisers about their rendered hours and work situations within the application.

Kiosk

During my third year at college, I had the opportunity to lead a group that developed a (kiosk) self-service ordering system for a local cafe using C#. We used Firebase as the database to provide real-time data to the management, including daily, weekly, and monthly sales and order tracking. The system was also capable of printing receipts after orders were placed.
